Fluxo CURL

Veja abaixo os comandos prontos para o seu terminal.

curl http://localhost:3060/account/pay-tags \\ -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN"
curl http://localhost:3060/account/pay-tags \\ -X POST \\ -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \\ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \\ -d '{ "name": "infra-deploy", "description": "Pay-tag mobile" }'
curl http://localhost:3060/account/pay-tags/{tag_id}/toggle \\ -X PATCH \\ -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN"
Sessões e tokens

O login seta cookie HttpOnly com TTL de 30 dias. Use Authorization ou x-session-token para executar comandos de pay-tags e receber payloads assinados.
